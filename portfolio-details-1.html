DIY Mortgage Website

Real-Time Mortgage Calculator & Market Tracker

This project focused on simplifying complex financial scenarios for homebuyers in the US market. The primary challenge was to integrate live housing feeds and process them into actionable insights without overwhelming the user.

Key Features & Implementation

  • Live Data Integration: Integrated live housing feeds to construct a real-time mortgage calculator.
  • Dynamic Dashboards: Created user-friendly interfaces that cut manual calculation overhead by 50%.
  • Actionable Insights: Derived market trends from US mortgage datasets to improve user decision-making efficiency by 35%.

The platform successfully scaled to over 500+ monthly users, demonstrating the demand for simplified, data-driven financial tools in the real estate sector.
